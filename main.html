Simple evolution simulator

The simulator displays inidividuals (‘bugs’) as little circles. Each bug has a 16 letter genome/ proteome/ metabolome (‘stuffome’). Stuffomes have a vocab of 20 letters, 10 synonymous pairs. If a bug has a specific survival sequence in its stuffome, it can eat and survive. Eating gives the bug Energy, and subtracts Energy from the common resource pool. If a bug has enough energy, it reproduces. The daughter bug has a certain chance to mutate. If there is not enough food available, a bug dies. You can view the population and resource level graphs.

Future plans include antibiotic resistance simulations
